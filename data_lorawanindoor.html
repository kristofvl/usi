LoRaWAN-Indoor
Nahshon Mokua Obiri and Kristof Van Laerhoven

Description

A comprehensive dataset collected from a multi-room office environment at the University of Siegen, Germany, using multiple LoRaWAN end devices and a central gateway. Each record includes device metadata (RSSI, SNR, spreading factor) alongside synchronized environmental parameters (temperature, humidity, pressure, COâ‚‚, and PM2.5). The dataset supports research on indoor wireless propagation, distance estimation, and environment-aware IoT modeling for next-generation applications.
